Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is WakeWave?
WakeWave is an AI-powered Drowsiness Detection System designed to monitor a driver’s alertness and provide warnings in real-time to prevent accidents caused by fatigue or drowsiness.
2. How does the Drowsiness Detection System work?
The system uses computer vision techniques to monitor the driver’s face, specifically eye movements, blinking patterns, and yawning. It analyzes these indicators in real-time using advanced AI models, alerting the driver when signs of drowsiness are detected.
3. What technology is used in WakeWave?
WakeWave uses a combination of OpenCV for real-time video analysis, dlib for facial landmark detection, and machine learning models to predict drowsiness. The system is integrated into a user-friendly web application built with Django.
4. Is the detection system accurate?
While no system is perfect, WakeWave has been tested and optimized to accurately detect drowsiness based on scientific research. The system continuously improves with updates to the underlying machine learning models.
5. What happens when the system detects drowsiness?
When drowsiness is detected, WakeWave immediately triggers an alert (visual and/or sound) to warn the driver. In future versions, we plan to include additional safety measures like automatic GPS alerts to a predefined emergency contact.
6. Can WakeWave be used on any device?
Yes, WakeWave can be accessed via our web application on any device with a camera, including laptops, smartphones, and tablets. It is platform-independent and runs on any browser that supports video input.
7. Do I need a constant internet connection to use the system?
No, WakeWave runs offline as well once loaded in your browser. However, some features like GPS integration and user data synchronization will require an internet connection.
8. Is my personal data safe?
We take data privacy very seriously. WakeWave only processes video data locally on your device for drowsiness detection. We do not store or share your video feed unless you enable cloud storage features for personal use.
9. How can I access the system?
You can access WakeWave through our web application. Simply visit our website, sign up for an account, and follow the setup instructions to begin using the drowsiness detection feature.
10. What are the future plans for WakeWave?
We are continuously working on improving the detection accuracy and adding new features such as:
  • Integration with wearable devices
  • Advanced analytics to track your driving habits
  • Customizable alert settings and notifications
  • Emergency contact alerts via GPS in case of drowsiness detection
11. How do I contact support if I face issues?
If you encounter any issues or need help, you can reach out to our support team at support@wakewave.com or visit our Support Page for more resources and troubleshooting guides.
